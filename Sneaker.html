PIZZA MENU

Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni, the superstar of pizza toppings, is a beloved cured meat that has become a culinary icon worldwide. Originating from Italy but with roots tracing back to Roman times, pepperoni has evolved into a staple topping for various dishes, especially pizza.

Neopolitan Pizza

Neapolitan pizza, an Italian culinary masterpiece, is a symbol of simplicity and tradition that has captured the hearts and taste buds of food enthusiasts worldwide. Its roots trace back to Naples, where this iconic dish emerged in the late 18th century.

Obaasima Pizza

Obaasima pizza is a culinary marvel that has captured the hearts and appetites of food enthusiasts worldwide. Known for its deep-dish crust, generous layers of toppings, and a reversed order of ingredients compared to traditional pizzas.

Ageygey Pizza

Ageygey pizza, an iconic variation of this beloved dish, captures the essence of Mediterranean flavors and culinary heritage. It's a delectable blend of fresh ingredients, unique spices, and a crust that carries a legacy of tradition.
